  • Build on the AHIMSA (Advanced Health Information Management & Social Analytics) stack, HDV enables care providers to enter vital patient / device data prior to, during & after the procedure

Patient Referral and Engagement Platform

  • Each patient transition point is tied to a stakeholder responsible for action.
